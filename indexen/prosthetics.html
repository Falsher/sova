Prosthetics

Often patients choose not to restore missing teeth in the dentition, especially if only one tooth is missing. They believe that other teeth may well cope with the masticatory load. And 32 teeth is a luxury. This is the wrong decision! As long as the teeth are shoulder to shoulder, they can withstand a lot. But just as the loss of one climber's ligament often leads to tragic consequences, so the loss of one tooth leads to reversible or irreversible changes in occlusion.

What do the specialists of the SoVa clinic offer?

We offer all kinds of modern dental prosthetics as a way to get rid of functional (difficulty chewing food), aesthetic (ugly smile) and somatic (health-related) problems.

What types of dental prosthetics are there?

In principle, in Kharkov and not only prosthetics are non-removable, removable and conditionally removable. The patient's teeth or dental implants can be used as a support by the orthopedist of the SoVa clinic. Going forward, let's say that the possibility of dental implants expands the boundaries of fixed prosthetics by almost 100%.

Crowns and bridges

These are classic examples of dental prosthetics. According to the WHO protocol, the crown covers the tooth when its crown (upper) part is destroyed by 50% or more, as well as any tooth that has undergone endodontic treatment. For the bridge it is necessary to process (prepare) teeth before and after defect. The support for the bridge prosthesis must be adequate. Our specialists use special load calculation tables and do not allow overloading of abutment teeth.

The material for such structures can be:

  • cobalt-chromium alloy (solid metal crowns)
  • all-cast crowns or bridges lined with ceramics - they used to be called "cermets"
  • metal-free ceramics
  • press ceramics
  • structures on a framework from zirconium oxide lined with ceramics

Dentures made of metal-free ceramics most completely restore the feeling of their teeth.

Veneers and luminaires

A variant of metal-free highly aesthetic prosthetics, which solves aesthetic, orthopedic and orthodontic problems at once with minimal loss of tooth tissue.

Removable prosthetics

Partial removable plate prostheses (PSPP) and full removable plate prostheses (PSPP)

Plate prostheses with a base of acrylic plastic or nylon are suitable for anyone who is thinking about highly aesthetic prosthetics, but can not yet afford it. The main thing is that they prevent the displacement of the abutment teeth, which is important in any fixed prosthesis. Complete removable plate prostheses (PSPP), despite their seeming simplicity, are the pinnacle of the skill of an orthopedic dentist. They can also be temporary or permanent depending on the upcoming treatment plan.

Conditionally removable prosthetics (clasp prostheses)

For patients who have absolute contraindications to implantation, but want to get a highly aesthetic and functional prosthesis, we can offer a clasp prosthesis with a clasp or lock fixation. What to choose depends on the specific case. The clasp allows to carry out prosthetics in general without processing of abutment teeth. The lock clasp has no visible fasteners. Everything is hidden in the prosthesis and abutment teeth, which are covered with metal-ceramic crowns of a special design. If you do not have one or more teeth, you feel uncomfortable eating, you are embarrassed by your smile and finally notice that your speech has changed - visit a dentist - orthopedist immediately. The doctor will select a suitable and affordable type of prosthesis. And very soon you will be able to fully eat, talk and smile.
