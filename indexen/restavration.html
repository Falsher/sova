Aesthetic restoration of teeth.

Aesthetic restoration of teeth Who does not dream of a beautiful and charming smile? But not always the condition of the teeth allows you to smile openly. Dark spots, chips, cracks in the enamel, etc. - Such problems, unfortunately, are very common. And they can be solved thanks to the aesthetic restoration of teeth, which allows you to recreate both the functionality of damaged teeth and their appearance in accordance with all the requirements of aesthetics: transparency, compliance with the shade of enamel and its luster.

In which cases aesthetic restoration of teeth is shown?

  • presence of three and diastemas (gaps between teeth)
  • chips, unsightly fillings and cracks in the enamel
  • lesions of tooth tissues - abrasion of enamel, fluorosis, hypoplasia)
  • teeth affected by caries
  • pigmentation that cannot be removed by bleaching)
  • staining of depulped teeth and discoloration during endodontic treatment
