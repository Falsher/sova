Surgical dentistry

In SoVaDent's dental practice we're implement full spectre of dental surgery, such as:

  • atraumatic removing of teeth of any complexity;
  • tooth saving operations: cystectomy with radectomy, hemisection;
  • dental bone graft;
  • collection and transportation of soft tissue grafts;
  • sinus lifting;
  • lip and tongue bridle plastics;
  • operation of dental implantation.

In surgical dentistry one of the most common manipulations is extraction of the teeth because of different reasons and indications.
We do atraumatic extraction of:
  • -impacted wisdom teeth wish complicated location and anatomy;
  • -extraction of hopeless teeth, which are not amenable to treatment;
  • -extraction of temporary teeth according to indications;
  • -extraction of movable teeth according to indications.

Pretty often are tooth saving operations:

  • - cystectomy with radectomy. This operation is directed to removing the inflammation sac together with the root apex, with tooth saving. Such an operation is carried out in case of unsuccessful endodontic treatment.
  • - hemisection applied on multirooted teeth and is the method of their saving. Essence of operation: amputation of ruined root with local pathology, which isn't amenable to conservative treatment.

One more important surgical manipulation is dental bone graft - this is the method of bone value reconstruction, which is necessary for the next step - implantation. This method is useful when implantation is planned in areas, where teeth have been missed for a long time and bone atrophied there.

Collection and transportation of soft tissue grafts are often used in our surgical practice. This manipulation implements in case of less soft tissues in the zone of implantation (after teeth removal, during the operation of dental bone graft and dental implantation). Also soft tissue grafts are used for closing of gum recession.

Sinus lifting.

Operation for maxillary sinus plastics, when it's needed to install dental implants in back areas of maxilla, but it's less bone tissue and close location of maxillary sinus. Usually held simultaneously with dental bone graft.

Lip and tongue bridle plastics is practiced in dental clinics SoVaDent. Implements correction of sizes and values of thick, heavy and incorrectly located bridles.

One of the most important and indispensable surgical manipulations for recovering the row of teeth is dental implantation - operation of inputting artificial titanium root into the bone for the next prosthetics on it.
