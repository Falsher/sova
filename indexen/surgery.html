Surgical dentistry

Tooth extraction in the SoVa clinic occurs only when there is no other way to save the tooth. We will never remove a wisdom tooth or any other tooth if it can be cured. However, if the patient seeks help very late and the situation is hopeless, then such a procedure is performed under quality and safe anesthesia painlessly and as quickly as possible.

Surgical treatment in emergencies is performed when there is:

  • acute inflammatory process that affects not only the teeth but also the soft tissues, the periosteum. In this case, there is a pronounced swelling of the face, asymmetry, pain, sometimes the presence of a fistula or purulent discharge.
  • trauma. Unfortunately, injuries do happen sometimes. A pinched tooth dislocation, when the tooth goes deep into the hole, a fracture of the root of the tooth, which can be determined only by X-ray - this is also a reason to remove the tooth
  • periodontitis. Movable teeth, which are easily loosened both back and forth and to the sides, are more than half the length of the root above the gum, are removed before prosthetics

  • preparation before orthodontic treatment. Sometimes, there is not enough space in the dentition to correct the bite, then you can "sacrifice" the second premolars, most often they are removed
  • removal of retinated, dystopian teeth, including wisdom teeth

Wisdom teeth removal in Kharkov

Wisdom comes with age, as do the teeth of "wisdom." They grow last, in the very corner of the jaw where the three large nerve trunks emerge. Because of this, even a slight inflammation that occurs when such teeth erupt, causes unpleasant and sometimes severe pain. This is not always a reason to part with a tooth! Pocket cleaning around the tooth and anti-inflammatory therapy often save the tooth from "extreme". But the wrong position of the "wisdom" tooth and the risk of trauma to the second molar in front of him, are direct indications for the removal of the "eight". The main thing to remember is that this and any other operation is not performed by specialists of our clinic without quality anesthesia. Our surgeons will perform all the necessary manipulations so as not to cause you discomfort.

Soft tissue surgery

Our dentists, surgeons and implantologists, so all the necessary operations to prepare for implantation - sinus lift, osteoplasty - they perform themselves. Procedures for surgical correction of the gingival margin (gingivoplasty), opening of abscesses, curettage (scraping) of gingival pockets, removal of neoplasms (cysts, granulomas) are also performed. Turning to the dental clinic "Owl" for help, you can be sure of a quality and qualified approach to surgical treatment of diseases and pathologies of the oral cavity. And the friendly and attentive staff will help you relax and forget about your pain as soon as possible.
