Implantology

Nowadays replacement of missing teeth by dental implants is the most modern method for renovation of integrity of the tooth row, chewing function and aesthetics.

The possibilities of dental implantation are infinite. Doesn't matter if the only one tooth is missing or several - prosthetics on implants are actual anyway.

Dental implant - is an artificial titanium root, which integrated at the bone tissue for the next prosthetics on it.

Operation of implantation is done under the local anesthesia and takes in general 60 minutes.

Nowadays replacement of missing teeth by dental implants is the most modern method for renovation of integrity of the tooth row, chewing function and aesthetics.

The possibilities of dental implantation are infinite. Doesn't matter if the only one tooth is missing or several - prosthetics on implants are actual anyway.

Dental implant - is an artificial titanium root, which integrated at the bone tissue for the next prosthetics on it.

Operation of implantation is done under the local anesthesia and takes in general 60 minutes.

Prosthetics on implants are:

  • separate, independent construction and this is the best alternative of classical non-removable prosthesis;

  • allow to avoid removable prosthesis in case with end defects;

  • conditionally removable prosthesis in case of fully loss teeth,
    thich are fixing on implants;

  • aesthetic rehabilitation of front teeth.

Methods:

  • ✔️immediate implantation after removing the tootht;
  • ✔️implantation in case of deficiency of bone, by conducting of dental bone graft;
  • ✔️using of short implants in case of deficiency of bone without additional operations;
  • ✔️production of temporary construction for the period of implant integration.
