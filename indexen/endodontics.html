Endodontic treatment

Such treatment is carried out carefully, accurately and only according to the modern endodontic protocol. After creating access to the pulp chamber, the doctor performs medical and instrumental treatment of the root canals, taking into account their anatomy (number, branches, drains) and topography (direction, location). Use of the modern endomotor allows to carry out processing of roots faster and more qualitatively, than in the mechanical way. Edomotor makes it possible to remove 90% of infected tissues, the remaining 10% are removed by irrigation (washing with a special syringe) with antiseptic solutions. Sealing of root canals with the help of modern certified materials ensures their reliable sealing. Proper handling reduces the risk of accidental tool breakage in the root canal.

The main goals of endodontics are:

  • cleaning of root canals, removal of carious and infected tissues (if the treatment is repeated, also remove the old filling material)
  • elimination of the inflammatory process
  • preparation of tooth canals for filling
  • installation of a permanent seal

It is very important to treat the root canals in a timely manner. This prevents the destruction and removal of the tooth, as well as the development of complications (pulpitis, periodontitis, etc.)

Doctors of our Medical Center have extensive experience in dealing with complex cases in endodontics. The center specializes in conservative treatment of cysts and cystogranules, extraction of instrument fragments, extraction of pins, sealing of impassable root canals, treatment of teeth with detected perforations. The professional level of doctors makes it possible to achieve successful results.
