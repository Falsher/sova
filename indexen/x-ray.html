Radiography

X-rays are indispensable in the work of the dentist, as it allows you to control the quality of treatment, for example, in filling and cleaning dental canals, determining the position of retained teeth, detecting latent caries, cysts, abscesses and other pathologies in tooth tissue, bone tissue, etc. it is applied to statement of the exact diagnosis and specification of volume and localization of works.

Aiming image of the tooth - X-ray in Kharkov

Dental office "SoVa" has a modern X-ray machine with low radiation levels. Competent specialists work with the equipment who qualitatively carry out X-ray diagnostics and reliably interpret its results. The finished intraoral image is used by the clinic's dentists to draw up a plan and X-ray control during treatment. Aiming of the tooth is performed according to the following indications:

  • caries, including hidden under a filling or prosthesis
  • endodontic treatment: pulpitis, periodontitis
  • study of the condition of the roots of the teeth, the number of canals
  • definition of cysts, granulomas
  • incorrect position of the growing tooth
  • the need to remove a tooth

It only takes a few minutes to get a targeted picture of a tooth. The procedure itself lasts a few seconds, which significantly reduces the radiation load on the patient. The modern equipment used in Sova dentistry is safe for the patient and as informative as possible for the doctor. Having made a sighting picture of a tooth in our office, you receive the correct diagnostics for the reasonable price.
