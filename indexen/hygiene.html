Prevention and hygiene

Therapeutic dental treatment begins with the prevention of caries. Professional brushing should be performed at least once a year, but preferably once every 6 months.

In our dental office for preventive cleaning of teeth are used:

  • Ultrasonic scalers. Ultrasonic vibrations in the aqueous medium break down the tartar, which is then easily separated from the tooth enamel. The procedure is painless and safe for teeth if performed correctly
  • Air - Flow. Cleaning of a pigmented plaque by an air-water-powder jet. Fine soda is used as a powder
  • Calcium-containing pastes (gels). They finish professional cleaning. The goal is to relieve sensitivity in the neck of the teeth

Remineralizing therapy or how to prevent caries

Specialists of the dental clinic "SoVa" perform a set of remineralizing therapy. It allows you to stop the carious process, restore damaged tooth enamel, strengthen healthy enamel, eliminate tooth sensitivity. Mandatory use of remtherapy in children, adult patients after braces and pregnant women - it will help prevent tooth decay. It is good to perform a set of remtherapy annually. People with a predisposition to periodontal disease - 2 times a year. The procedure is very simple - in the office will be obtained casts, they made an individual kappa. The patient applies the remineralizing gel or paste to such an individual mouthpiece, and after brushing his teeth puts it on (usually at night).

Sealing of fissures in Kharkov

Another preventive procedure is sealing the fissures. The Owl Clinic performs sealing of tooth fissures - a procedure of sealing those areas of the tooth where the enamel is not strong enough and bacteria can cause irreparable damage. The sealant evens out all the irregularities of the tooth surface, strengthens the enamel due to the release of fluoride, does not wear off when brushing teeth. Sealants can be applied to teeth that have just erupted or to permanent teeth several years after eruption, if they are not prone to caries. If there is a sensitivity of the teeth, then you can apply fluoride varnish on the teeth. The complex of preventive measures carried out in the SoVa clinic is a way to a healthy smile! Regular professional cleaning allows you to find any changes in the condition of the teeth and gums in the early stages and prevent the development of complications.
